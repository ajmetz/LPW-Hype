I believe we can all benefit from one another's
knowledge and experience,
when we knowledge-share.

London Perl & Raku Workshop talks,
are a great way to do that!

You'll find the latest info on this year's talks here:
https://www.londonperlworkshop.com/talks

You can also contact us to submit your own talk proposal.

Include in your email:

  • Title
  • Summary
  • Duration

We typically have 20 minute or 40 minute slots,
and then 5 minute lightening talk slots,
and are tolerant of any variation, having already greenlit a 40-50min talk.
